THE WHY

Over the years with little resources available, most designers learnt design by "trial and error" without a good understating of the principles behind every action taken. Basically, we are giving what some of us never had, a tutor to guide and teach them, community of designers like themselves to collaborate and share ideas together.

More focus on digital products has been placed on coding, a sample research by Temidayo and myself realized some students who applied to these coding academy actually has interest and passion for design but little design academy offering affordable tutoring and guidance.

In order to guide prospective creatives in making immersive experience and designing beautiful interfaces for the future products this platform was initiated to tutor designers making sure no one ever walked alone in their journey.

With UX THE CLASS, the Nigerian design ecosystem will be known to release only world class experience for digital products.

Ehizojie Ihayere

Founder, Design Principal
