THE VISION

When this initiative was formed, it had one goal in mind which was to enhance the creativity of Nigerian digital designers.

The ecosystem has never needed more designers than now and as such we have a big role to play in preparing prospective designers to fill in the spaces. With our first cohort, we began with a sizable amount of 20 students and due to our demand, we rebuilt our system to hold about 1,000's and by the next 5 years we would have been able to have trained more than 100,000 professional designers.

With our world class tutors and more designers from our academy in the space, we believe digital products will have better experiences which will improve usability of products.

You can be a part of this great initiative by working with us in making this vision come true. There is a lot to be done and with us working together, I believe we can make this come true.

Welcome to a new journey.

Ehizojie Ihayere

Founder, Design Principal
